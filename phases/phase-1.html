Exploration

Break a simple thing.

Pick one:

A chai stall Your college canteen A bus stop

Map it visually. Include:

  • People
  • Objects
  • Invisible elements (rules, habits, money, time)
Constraint Do not write first. Only draw.

You may find yourself drawing in a way that looks familiar.

Disruption

Remove one element.

  • No cash
  • No seating
  • No peak hour

Observe what changes.
