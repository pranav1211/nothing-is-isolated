Design

Design the system, not the visuals.

Focus on:

  • Interactions
  • Incentives
  • Feedback loops

The visuals come later. The behavior comes from the structure.

⚡
Final prompt
If someone enters this system, what will it make them do — whether they want to or not?
What you should notice now

Something has likely shifted.

If you've stayed with this honestly, you may:

  • Pause before jumping to solutions
  • Notice patterns where you previously saw isolated events
  • Question things that used to feel "normal"

More importantly — you may no longer feel comfortable designing something without thinking about what it will do to people.

Moving forward Every interface, poster, space, or message you create will push behavior in some direction. What will this make people do?
