Before you begin

Feel the loop.

Before mapping anything, notice this. Pick one:

  • Open a social media app
  • Delay a task you should be doing
  • Check your phone without thinking

Now pause. Ask:

  1. What just triggered that?
  2. What did I get from it?
  3. Why will I likely do it again?
Notice You are inside a loop.
Contrast — A mostly linear system

How food moves through your body.

  • It enters
  • Gets processed
  • Leaves

This is a forward-moving system.

Compare to your earlier experience

Some systems feed back into themselves.

Some systems move forward and exit. Others keep feeding back into themselves. You've already seen both — whether you noticed it or not.

Exploration

Find loops.

Look for repeating patterns:

Procrastination Social media use Group discussions

Map cycles:

Action → Result → Reinforcement → Repeat

  • or — Amplify it
